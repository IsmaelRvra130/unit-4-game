Crystal game!

You will be givin a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below.
By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random
numbers,you lose the game if your total score goes above
the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts,the game will change the
balues of each crystal.
